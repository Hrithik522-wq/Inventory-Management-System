# 🔐 StockXpert Login & Management Guide

To fulfill your request for absolute autonomy over your system, I have removed all "default" credentials and provided you with two simple, one-click tools to manage your logins.

## 1. Setting up your Admin Account
By default, the admin credentials were hardcoded. You can now change them easily:
- Double-click **`Setup-Admin.bat`** in the project folder.
- Enter your preferred **Username** and **Password**.
- The system will immediately update your configuration.

## 2. Adding User Accounts
To create a regular login for yourself or others:
- Double-click **`Add-User.bat`**.
- Enter the **Name**, **Email**, and **Password**.
- You can then use these to log in immediately on the main page.

## 3. Manual Entry
The login pages for both Users and Admins are now **completely blank** by default. You will no longer see "HR@gmail" or "admin123" pre-filled—you must enter your custom credentials every time for security.

### 🚀 **Ready to launch?**
Run **`Run-StockXpert.bat`** to start the servers, then use your custom logins!
